# Thermodynamics — Elementary and Advanced

**Read online: [learnthermo.org](https://learnthermo.org)**

A free, rigorous course on thermodynamics, from the foundational principles
to advanced and modern applications. Written for undergraduates and graduate
students, and freely readable in the browser (French and English), with
KaTeX-rendered mathematics and downloadable per-chapter PDFs.

Each lesson is paired with a library of worked exercises — statements,
hints, and full solutions — also available as a single downloadable PDF per
theme.

## Contents so far

**Part I — Foundations (undergraduate level)**

- **Introduction and history** — motivation and the historical development
  of thermodynamics.
- **Fundamental concepts** — state variables, equilibrium, and equations of
  state.
- **First and second laws** — energy conservation, reversibility, and
  entropy.
- **Fundamental relations and changes of variables** — the mathematical
  structure of thermodynamic potentials and Legendre transforms.
- **Calorimetric and thermoelastic coefficients**, **phase transitions**,
  and **heat engines** — applying the formalism to gases, phase diagrams,
  and cyclic machines.

**Part II — Structure and advanced topics**

- **Differential geometry in thermodynamics** — a more formal, geometric
  view of thermodynamic spaces.
- **Maxwell's demon**, **endoreversible cycles**, **thermoelectricity**, and
  **non-equilibrium thermodynamics** — classic and modern extensions beyond
  equilibrium.
- **Climate and thermodynamics**, **quantum thermodynamics** — applications
  connecting the formalism to contemporary physics.
